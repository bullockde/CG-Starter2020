    The Plan

    We Hope To Create A Website With Integrity

    We are excited to have the opportunity to present our plan. We hope to be able to help The Historical Society of Tacony with it's web properties, including the Historical Society website, and secondary web properties as needed.

    Website Up To Date

    The Historical Society of Tacony would benefit from a new website. There are several critical reasons, but the security concerns seem to be the most pressing. The present website code is very old, and much less secure then it should be. The site is highly susceptible to compromise because the code is not up to date.

    Internet Marketing

    Although it is not the intention to acquire a monetary gain, the sale of product related to the organizations brand adds a special interactive feature, is valuable to the community, and is a viable method of promotion. A call to action donation function is also part of the features plan.

    Web Management

    Part of our process includes continued development, growth, and management. Our responsibilities include assistance with interactive posts, graphics and photography assistance, and technical maintenance such as backup, security, typical code update maintenance, hosting, domain registration, and SSL management.

    A Website Designed To Be A Valuable Asset

    The present Historical Society of Tacony website is insecure, and much of the content is hosted on (Blogger) which is a platform that could be slated for discontinue in the near future. We are eager to create a valuable asset that brings interest, and value to the Community. Although we will create many features we will be focused primarily on these two areas.

    Engagement Based Design

    Through the use of well thought out design and intuitive user interface structures, we will attempt to engage visitors and keep them interested. The flow of the website will be designed for easy navigation.

    Interactive Features

    Using exciting features like interactive maps, animated historic time-lines, and a rich historical information reference we hope to make The Historical Society of Tacony website fun to go to.

    Our Process

    Grindingly Thorough Research And Design

    Learning and research starts the process. Meetings and gathering of client resources follow, then design concepts are presented along with copy-writing, graphics and design standards. Along the way we may be utilizing out photography staff, and artists. Several revisions are likely drawn up before we have just the right look. A lot of technical work is involved including work with Google and other search engines to make sure the site is optimized. Afterwards we will be performing regular maintenance and management duties that might include security, backup, SEO, and digital marketing efforts.

    Research

    Learning, research, discovery meetings, and gathering assets we will need. We are going to be learning so that we can develop a structure for the site, and represent your topic the right way.

    Concept

    Usually many design concepts are tried out before we find the design that works well. The logical structure of the site is put together based upon what is important to you.

    Development

    A logical structure is made that defines your site. This may be products, services, we may be designing for lead generation or for finding information easily.

    Deployment

    After revisions are approved and the site is the way you like it to be, we point your domain name and provide some necessary administrative work with Google.
